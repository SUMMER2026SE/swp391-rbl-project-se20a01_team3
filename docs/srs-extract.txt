1 INTRODUCTION
1.1. Purpose
This document describes the functional and non-functional requirements for the Bee Academy online learning platform. It serves as a common basis for the development team, supports the testing team in building test cases (Unit, Integration, System), and acts as a reference for scope confirmation with stakeholders throughout the SDLC.
1.2. System Scope
Bee Academy operates on a collaboration and revenue-sharing model between the platform and Teachers. The system includes:
Student Portal: Search, purchase, pay, and study at an individual pace; take exams and download certificates.
Parent Portal: Monitor child's progress, handle payments, and contact Teachers through linked accounts.
Teacher Portal: Create courses, exams, and materials; update question banks; grade assignments; and view payout history.
Admin Panel: Approve courses, handle complaints, perform manual payout cycles, and manage user accounts.
Technology: RESTful API backend, VNPay/MoMo integration, and an AI Engine for chat support and roadmap suggestions.
Out of Scope: Livestreaming, fixed schedules, and automatic transfers to Teachers (payouts are manual).
